Passionate about Science, Engineering and Research!

But doing Science is not my only interest!

Indeed, I have always been attracted by flying machines and fast racing cars and motorbikes. I earned my wings on a ultralight machine when I was 17, before I could even drive a car, and I have spent hours in the hangar, trying to figure out differences between planes. Very unsurprisingly, I believe this innate attraction has been the main driver in my career up to today. Beside aircraft and helicopters, I have always been fond of motorsport racing!
